Why census is important


  • Helps in policy-making (education, healthcare, infrastructure)
  • Determines resource allocation
  • Affects electoral representation
  • Supports economic planning
  • Healthcare Planning
  • Disaster Management & Relief Planning
  • Foreign Investments & Business Growth









A census is an official process of collecting, analyzing, and recording data about a country's population, including demographics, housing, and economic conditions.

The census helps in policy-making, resource allocation, electoral representation, and economic planning by providing accurate population data.

The Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS) is responsible for organizing and managing the census.

Yes, all data is kept confidential and used only for statistical purposes. The Pakistan Bureau of Statistics follows strict data protection laws.

If your household is missed, you can contact the census helpline or visit the official website to report it.
